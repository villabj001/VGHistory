    • The Video game Timeline

    1967

    First Console

    Ralph Baer creates what is known as the Brown Box. This allowed you to play games like Pong, Checkers, 5 variety sports games and a game using a light gun

    1977

    Most notable early console

    One of the first at-home consoles is released, the Atari 2600 Worldwide unit sales reach 27,640,000

    1980

    Arcade popular

    Pac-Man starts showing up across pizzerias across the nation and ends up selling 350,000 arcade boxes with $2 billion in revenue. This starts a boom of arcade cabinets being sold all over the world

    1985 & 1989

    Nintendo Entertainment System and Gameboy

    The Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) is released worldwide racking up 62 million units sold. Nintendo also releases a handheld console called the Gameboy which had even more units sold with 118 million

    1993

    Violence debate

    Mortal Kombat a fighting game with heavy gore is brought up among parents as it could make kids more violent due to playing such a game. This puts all other game into the question and sparks a huge debate

    1995 & 1996

    Polygon Generation

    Sony and Nintendo were supposed to team up on a disc based 3D console but Nintendo rejected Sony's idea. In retaliation, Sony releases the PlayStation with sales reaching 104 million. Nintendo in return releases the Nintendo 64 which only sold 34 million copies

    2000

    Best console?

    Sony releases the PlayStation 2 which is the most popular and most sold console of all time. Even to this day, no console has passed it in sales which it has 157 million

    2003

    PC Revolution

    The PC gaming scene has not been as big as the console scene has but starting from here on out, PC starts becoming a better way to experience games. Valve releases Steam which is a platform for developers to distribute their games

    2005

    Microsoft Success

    The Xbox 360 is released and is a better console than the original Xbox in every way. The original flopped but this current console is a core memory of many gamers due to games like Call of Duty and Halo. The 360 sold 84 million units

    2008

    Social Gaming

    World of Warcraft becomes the most popular MMO in gaming history and it's community pioneers the current social aspect of gaming

    2009-Now

    Mobile Craze

    With the relesae of the iOS App Store, developers started releasing not only useful applications but also games. This started a whole new way to play games on the go and to this day mobile gaming is still increasingly popular
